# Count Down

## User Journey

### Pick a timezone (Toronto or Vancouver)

Left half of screen: Vancouver
Right half of screen: Toronto

Showing local time when hovered, and expand a bit

### Count down

Simple number count down from hours to seconds
Play some music while we wait

In the last 10 seconds:
- Play beeping sound per second
- Change color of the countdown text
- Stop at 0 and play fireworks for 1 minute
